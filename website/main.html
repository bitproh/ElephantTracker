About Us

Our project, the Elephant Detection and Surveillance System, aims to monitor and protect elephants through cutting-edge technology. By integrating real-time GPS tracking and advanced analytics, we provide insights into elephant movements to help mitigate human-elephant conflicts and support wildlife conservation efforts.

Objectives:

- To develop a system that tracks elephants in real-time, providing location data to prevent human-wildlife conflicts.

- To develop a user-friendly interface for monitoring elephant movement data, enabling wildlife officers, researchers, and conservationists to make informed decisions quickly and effectively.

To create a database that stores elephant movement data and patterns, facilitating long-term research, analysis, and conservation strategies, while also enabling public awareness and engagement.

-To create alerts for authorities when elephants are near human settlements or vulnerable areas.

Motivation:

-Rising human-elephant conflict due to urbanization and agricultural encroachment.

-The increasing threat to elephant populations from poaching and habitat loss.

-The need for innovative solutions to ensure peaceful coexistence between humans and elephants while preserving the species for future generations.
